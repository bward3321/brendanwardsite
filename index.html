About Me

The short version.

I'm Brendan. I run Growtoro, where we help businesses generate leads and grow without burning money on ads that don't work. Cold email, AI-powered outreach, data sourcing, full-stack campaign execution — we've done it for hundreds of companies.

Before Growtoro I built and exited two companies. Started hustling as a teenager doing lawn care and building websites — that scrappy energy never left.

These days I'm deep in AI. Not the buzzword version — the practical version. Building systems that actually save people time and make them money. I also build web apps, content properties, automated social media systems, AI-generated videos and music, and whatever else sounds interesting on a Tuesday afternoon.

I recently became a dad, which has been the most humbling and rewarding thing I've ever done — and I include exiting two companies in that comparison.
