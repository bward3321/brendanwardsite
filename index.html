About Me

The short version.

I'm Brendan. I run Growtoro, where we help businesses generate leads and grow without burning money on ads that don't work. Cold email, AI-powered outreach, data sourcing, full-stack campaign execution — we've done it for hundreds of companies.

Before Growtoro I built and exited two companies. Started hustling as a teenager doing lawn care and building websites for local businesses — that scrappy energy never left.

These days I'm deep in practical AI. Not the buzzword version. Building systems that actually save people time and make them money. Web apps, content properties, automated social media systems, AI-generated video and music — whatever sounds interesting on a Tuesday afternoon.

I live in the Hudson Valley with my wife Jaimie, our daughter Eliza, and our Portuguese Water Dog Nellie. I recently became a dad, which has been the most humbling and rewarding thing I've ever done — and I include exiting two companies in that comparison.

When I'm not building things, I'm reading obsessively, traveling, watching sports, exploring new tech, or creating weird and wonderful AI content.
